Welcome to uBOL-Expanded

An unofficial fork of uBlock Origin Lite with additional filter lists.

What is this?

This is an expanded version of uBlock Origin Lite. It includes additional filter lists out-of-the-box, giving you more flexibility for ad, tracker, and annoyance blocking without manually compiling your own extension.

All core features, logic, and base filter lists are inherited directly from uBO Lite.

Getting started

Open the dashboard by clicking the ⚙ icon in the popup to manage your filter lists and settings.

You can freely toggle on or off any of the included extra filter lists from the Filter lists tab. Some additional lists are turned off by default to conserve resources, but they are fully available whenever you need them.
